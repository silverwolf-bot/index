Chat Gacha:

- Only the Administrators can activate or deactivate a gacha with /manage gacha .

- You can make the bot ignore channels with /manage channel .

- You can choose to redirect the drops messages with /manage redirect .

- To make a gacha work in a specific channel, use /manage separation .

- To enable trading in the server, use /manage trading .

- Together with activation, you will receive the commands: /pity , /inventory , /missing , /top , /trade for each gacha.

Silver Wolf has these commands:

- /banners now - Check the current and future banners from Honkai: Star Rail

- /wiki - Check information on any Character, Lightcone, or Relic Set from Honkai: Star Rail together with materials needed for pre-farming.

- /builds - Check how you can get the most out of your stamina by knowing how to build your characters.

- /calculator - Calculate how many pulls you will have until the selected banners end. This command works for:
   Stellar Jades (Honkai: Star Rail) / Primogems (Genshin Impact) / Crystals (Honkai Impact 3rd).

Help command:

- You can view all of Silver Wolf's commands and how the gacha works using /help .

Mini-Games:

- /tic tac toe - Play against your friends or even against yourself in the Tic Tac Toe game.

-  /rock paper scissors - Play the Rock, Paper, Scissors game against a friend.

Feedback:

- I am always planning to improve the bot, so if you have feedback, please don't hesitate to use the command /feedback or join the Support Server to directly send the developer suggestions, what you like, or even what you dislike about the bot.
